What is CRIDA

Pakistan's Central Identity Authority

A unified, secure platform that connects every Pakistani citizen to their official government identity record.

CRIDA — the Citizen Registration & Identity Database Authority — is the Government of Pakistan's primary institution responsible for the registration, maintenance, and verification of civilian identity data across the country.

Established to modernize Pakistan's identity infrastructure, CRIDA maintains a centralized, secure database encompassing national identity records, biometric data, family registrations, travel documents, and civil certificates for all citizens and registered residents.

Our mandate extends from issuing CNICs and passports to maintaining family tree records, managing criminal watchlists, and providing real-time identity verification services to government agencies, law enforcement, and authorized third parties.

Through our digital Citizen Self-Service Portal, Pakistanis can now access their records, download official documents, track applications, and request information updates — all without visiting a physical office.

Central Identity Database

A unified repository of every registered citizen's identity record, biometrics, and civil documentation — secured with enterprise-grade encryption.

Verified & Trusted

CRIDA records are the authoritative source of truth for identity verification across all government and financial institutions in Pakistan.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Identity Services

From issuance of national documents to civil record management — everything you need, digitally accessible.

CNIC — National Identity Card

Apply for a new CNIC, renew an expiring card, report a lost card, or update personal information. Track your application status in real time through our portal.

Passport Issuance & Renewal

Submit new passport applications or renewal requests for ordinary, official, and diplomatic passports. Download your application slip and track processing at every stage.

Driving License Management

Apply for learner's permits, motor vehicle licenses, and heavy transport licenses. Check test results and download your license once approved.

Birth Registration

Register newborns and obtain official birth certificates — a prerequisite for CNIC issuance, school enrollment, and passport applications.

Marriage Certificates

Register civil marriages and obtain official Nikah and marriage certificates recognized by all government and financial institutions.

Family Tree & Relations

View your complete family record including parents, spouse, children, and extended relations as maintained in the national register.

How to Access Your Records

Get your government identity documents and records in four easy steps through our secure digital portal.

1
Verify Identity

Enter your 13-digit National Identity Number and your CRIDA Citizen ID to authenticate your access to the portal.

2
Access Records

View your complete profile — personal information, documents, applications, family tree, and notifications — all in one place.

3
Download Documents

Generate official PDFs of your CNIC, passport, driving license, birth certificate, and other civil records instantly.

4
Track & Update

Monitor application statuses, submit data correction requests, file complaints, and receive real-time notifications from CRIDA.
